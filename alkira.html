Alkira Gardens

Alkira Gardens is a newly established state-of-the-art aged care facility providing general and dementia care as well as respite care for 90 residents. 

The facility is conveniently located in the heart of Miranda and within walking distance to Miranda Westfield Shopping Centre and train station.

Alkira Gardens is concept designed and equipped with the latest in aged care amenities. Resident rooms and the communal areas are spacious, comfortable and tastefully decorated.  There are single rooms with ensuite, double rooms with ensuite and double rooms with shared bathrooms to choose from. Lifts provide easy access to different levels. The facility is designed for flexibility with ‘easy living’ features for residents such as wide access doors, 24-hour nursing call system, full air-conditioning, in room telephone outlet for subscription, internet room, easy access to under covered outdoor areas & beautifully landscaped gardens.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Alkira Gardens is an exciting new addition to the Shire’s aged care community. It is committed to enhancing the wellbeing of residents by providing high quality accommodation and person centred care.
