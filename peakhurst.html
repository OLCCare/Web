Peakhurst Lodge

Peakhurst Lodge has been operated by The Sisters of Our Lady of China since 1984. The facility provides nursing, personal care and diversional activities to anyone requiring high care and or low care nursing. Placement is regardless of race, religion, colour or creed.

After going through major construction and renovation in the past few years, Peakhurst Lodge now cares for 71 high care residents and 39 low care residents in a safe, comfortable and home like environment with a team of caring and dedicated staff.

The 71-bed high care section has been extended and fully renovated including a 14-bed secure dementia specific section. All our 39 low care hostel beds are single rooms with ensuite.

Each room has quality furnishings, built-in wardrobes, a bar fridge and tea making facilities. Most general section rooms have a balcony opening to the courtyard. A 12-bed section offers a secured environment to cater for the special needs of dementia residents.

 

 

 

 

 

 

The courtyard and gardens have been beautifully landscaped, they provide a safe, peaceful outdoor environment for residents to enjoy with family and other residents.

Lifts provide easy access to both levels of the building. There are a number of activity rooms, lounge rooms and dining rooms in each of the sections. A spacious general activity room and a chapel are built for all residents to enjoy activities, church service and prayers.
