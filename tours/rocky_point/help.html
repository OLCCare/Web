Why Can't I See The Virtual Tour?
To view this virtual tour you will need to have the Flash plug-in installed in the browser you are viewing the virtual tour in. Without the Flash plug-in the virtual tour will not load and you will not see the virtual tour image. To download the latest version of the Flash plug-in for your browser click on the Flash logo below and download the installer from the web site for your operating system. Once the download is complete, run the installer and follow all installation instructions. You can also check which version of Flash you have installed by clicking here.

If you are using dial up internet access with a 56k modem to view the virtual tour then each image may take a little while to load. The status bar will tell you how much of the virtual tour image has loaded.

How Do I Use The Virtual Tour?
Place your mouse over the virtual tour image, hold down your mouse button and drag the cursor left, right, up or down to rotate around the room or area. You can also use the auto tour buttons located on the tool bar to navigate around the environment and you can stop the tour at any time by clicking on the virtual tour image or the stop button. To zoom in and out simply click the + and - buttons on the tool bar.

What Is A Hot Spot?
A 'Hot Spot' is a link which is mapped onto the virtual tour image itself and allows you to jump to another room or area in the virtual tour. Hot spots allow you to virtually 'walk' around the property by linking virtual tour images (or nodes) together. When you move your cursor over a hot spot the hot spot will become active and a small text hint will be displayed to tell you exactly where you are about to go. Hot spots will only appear in the virtual tour if the virtual tour has hot spot functionality included.
