Ruby Manor

The 90-bed modern hostel was established in 2007. Set amidst beautifully landscaped gardens, Ruby Manor provides 90 low care beds with Ageing in Place including a 30-bed unit specialising in care for dementia residents.

Respite care is also available. It is close to public transport and local amenities. The facility can be easily accessed via the M5.

The building is fully air-conditioned. There are 60 single rooms with ensuites and 15 double rooms with shared ensuites. Each room has quality furnishings and built-in wardrobes. All general rooms all have tea-making facilities.

Set in pleasant surroundings, there are three wings on each floor of the four-storey Manor, with the top three floors being occupied by residents.
