Pembroke Lodge

Established in 2005 and located in Campbelltown Shire, the 108 bed modern facility provides a full spectrum of services with 26 high care beds and 82 low care beds, including two secure dementia units.

The modern facility is specially designed and built to ensure a safe, comfortable and home like environment for all residents at a high standard. Respite care is also available at the facility.

The building is fully air-conditioned. In the low care section, there are 56 single rooms with ensuites, 18 single rooms with shared ensuites and 4 double rooms with shared ensuites. In the high care section, there are 13 spacious double rooms with shared ensuites. Each room has quality furnishings, built-in wardrobes and tea-making facilities.
