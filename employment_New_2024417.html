Employment

The Sisters of Our Lady of China has a staffing profile that reflects contemporary Australian society – multicultural and ethnically diverse.

We always consider people are our greatest assets. The Sisters of Our Lady of China has been dedicated to attracting and retaining staff, as well as recognising people’s rights.

We always fully support our staff to undergo various forms of training and learning. This commitment ensures our team are able, through increased knowledge and skills, to improve the standard of care and service delivery to our residents.

The organisation holds recognition events each year to celebrate our achievements and The Incentive Award program rewards our staff who work above and beyond the minimum.

Current Vacancies

 

Peakhurst Lodge - Registered Nurse

We are seeking a registered nurse with a passion of aged care to join our team.

You will be providing safe care with an emphasis on compassionate care and a holistic approach through assessment, planning, implementation and evaluation of nursing care.

You will be responsible for ensuring the highest standards of person centred care and administer medication, maintain high quality care and documentation, complete care plans and assessments and take an active role in getting to know patients and their families.

Successful candidates will have:

• A current AHPRA registration

• Experience and skills in caring for the elderly

• Able to maintain a safe and high standard of practice

• Demonstrate excellent written, verbal and interpersonal communication skills

• Ability to work autonomously

If you are interested in this opportunity and email your resume to cheryl@ourladyofchina.org.au or phone 02 9533 4089 for further information and a confidential discussion.
