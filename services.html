Services

With extensive experience in aged care, we pride ourselves on the individualised quality care we provide to our residents by our highly qualified professional staff.

Promoting the dignity and respect for human life at all stages, extending the hand of a Samaritan and caring with love, hope and faith, are all integral elements of our work.

Our residents enjoy a friendly, caring community atmosphere that is fostered by the caring philosophy of the management and staff and enhanced by the modern design and amenities of our facilities.

We strive to provide services which meet the needs of our residents and aim to help them maintain and improve their health through providing a variety of recreational activities, gentle exercises and Diversional therapies.

We also provide pastoral care to meet the spiritual needs of people of a variety of faiths and provide individual emotional support as well as ceremonies to mark spiritual occasions.

Elderly people who have a mental illness, challenging behaviours or dementia are well catered for in specialist services with trained staff and tailored activities and therapies.

 

Features Include

  • Fully air conditioned high standard accommodation in a safe and comfortable home like environment
  • Numerous lounge rooms with large flat screen TVs, quiet sitting rooms and dining rooms
  • Beautifully landscaped gardens
  • Spacious activity room
  • Chapel
  • On site hairdressing salon
  • Delicious, freshly cooked nutritious meals and refreshments catering for individual dietary needs
  • Diversional therapy and lifestyle programme including arts, crafts, music, games, cards, regular bus excursions, social outings, religious services and individualised dementia activities
  • 24 hour individualised nursing care
  • Mini-Libraries
  • Cleaning & Laundry Services
